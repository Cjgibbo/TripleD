Where It Began

Sixteen years in print.
One clear next step.

Delight Digital Designs was created from a simple realization: many small businesses need practical, affordable websites that actually work for them.

After spending more than sixteen years working in the printing industry, I became deeply familiar with how businesses present themselves to customers through printed materials — books, catalogs, magazines, and marketing pieces. During that time I began building Excel tools to solve pricing challenges inside the company. Those spreadsheets eventually grew into complex calculators that automated production estimates.

That problem-solving process led naturally into web development. When the company needed e-commerce and website solutions, I began building sites and integrating those same calculator systems online. What started as a technical project quickly became something I genuinely enjoyed.

"Building websites combines two things I value: logical problem solving and creative design. A well-built website should help a business communicate clearly, organize information, and make it easier for customers to connect."

— Founder, Delight Digital Designs

Living in a small town also made something very clear to me. Many local businesses simply need reliable websites that are straightforward, professional, and easy to manage — without unnecessary complexity or high costs.

Delight Digital Designs was created to provide exactly that. The goal is simple: build practical websites that help businesses present themselves professionally online, connect with their customers, and grow over time.

Every project is an opportunity to keep learning, keep improving, and help bring someone else's idea to life on the web.
